I'm a SIEM Engineer at Volkswagen Financial Services, where I operate and develop on an IBM QRadar estate of over 23,000 log sources. My work covers the full detection lifecycle — from log source onboarding and pipeline engineering to correlation rule development, tuning, and incident response. Before moving into enterprise SIEM, I spent three years at a Canadian MSSP building and leading a security operations team across multiple client environments. I care about detection that is precise, maintainable, and grounded in real adversary behaviour.
