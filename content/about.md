## Hugo Miranda

I'm a SIEM Engineer and Detection Engineer focused on building reliable, scalable detection infrastructure for organisations that can't afford to get it wrong.

My work sits at the intersection of threat detection, log engineering, and compliance — primarily NIS2 and DORA. I design detection pipelines using Sigma rules, implement SIEM architectures that don't collapse under real-world log volumes, and help security teams move from reactive alerting to structured, evidence-based detection.

**Core focus areas:**

- Detection-as-code (Sigma, MITRE ATT&CK mapping)
- SIEM architecture and tuning (Splunk, Elastic, Microsoft Sentinel)
- NIS2 / DORA compliance for SMBs and critical infrastructure operators
- Log pipeline engineering and data normalisation

I operate as an independent consultant through **ne0tw0**, working with organisations across Europe.
